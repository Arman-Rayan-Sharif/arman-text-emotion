## Introduction

This repo contains information about Arman Rayan Sharif company's exclusive data for the field of emotion recognition from text.

our dataset contains 7 classes:


- Happiness 
- Sadness 
- Anger 
- Fear 
- Wonder 
- Hatred 
- Other
